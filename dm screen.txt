> CREW ACTIONS
	- Sail: DC 10 str (water vehicles), give ship speed unit, half on failure
	- Helmsman: required if noted in ship stats, otherwise needed to make checks for precise manoeuvers, str or dex water vehicles
	- Operate: use any ship object not related to sailing or helming, manning weapons is main use 
	- Command: DC 10 cha, success adds +2 to ship initative
	
CONDITIONS
- Blinded
	> auto fail sight checks
	> attack rolls against have advantage, own attacks disadvantage
- Charmed
	> cant attack, target or harm charmer
	> charmer has advantage on social interactions with creature
- Deafened
	> auto fail hearing checks
- Frightened
	> disadvantage on checks and attacks while source is in LOS 
	> cant willingly move closer to source
- Grappled
	> speed becomes 0
	> may be able to attack depending on grapple
- Incapacitated
	> cant take actions or reactions
- Invisible
	> impossible to see, heavily obscured for hiding, can be detected by noise or tracks
	> attacks rolls agaisnt have disadvantage, own attacks advantage
- Paralyzed
	> incapacitated, cant speak or move
	> auto fail str and dex checks
	> attack rolls against have advantage
	> any melee attack within 5ft is auto crit
- Petrified
	> transformed into stone, weight increases x10, ceases aging
	> incapacitated, unaware of surroundings
	> attacks againts have advantage
	> auto fail str and dex checks
	> resistance to all dmg
	> immune to poison and disease, effects active before are paused, not healed
- Exhaustion
	> lvl 1, disadvantage on checks
	> lvl 2, speed halved
	> lvl 3, disadvantage on attacks and saves
	> lvl 4, hp max halved
	> lvl 5, speed 0 
	> lvl 6, death 
- Poisoned
	> disadvantage on attacks and checks
- Prone
	> disadvantage on attacks, melee attacks against have advantage, ranged disadvantage
- Restrained
	> speed 0
	> attacks against have advantage, own attacks disadvantage
	> disadvantage on dex saves
- Stunned
	> incapacitated, can barely speak
	> auto fail str and dex saves
	> attacks against have advantage
- Unconscious
	> incapacitated
	> drops whatever theyre holding and falls prone
	> auto fail str and dex saves
	> melee attacks within 5ft auto crit
	
* Grappling
- can use attack action to make special melee attack
- target must be no more than one size larger than you
- needs one free hand
- contest attackers Athletics vs targets Athletics or Acrobatics
- can attempt escape with an action, contest
- can drag grappled creatures for half speed, unless 2 sizes smaller
- shoving is the same, but pushes back or down instead

TRAVEL

- can travel 8 hours at forced march pace per day. 
- each additional hour beyond 8, must make a CON save DC 10 + 1 for each hour over 8. 
- on fail, gain one lvl of exhaustion
* vehicles, limited to speed of vessel, each creature can travel for 16 hours before tiring, and vehicle may even travel for 24 hours if enough crew is available

RESTING

- Short rest
	> at least 1 hour long
	> do nothing but eat, drink, read, tend wounds
	> can spend one or more hit dice and heal the rolled amount + CONmod 
	
- Long rest
	> extended downtime, at least 8 hours
	> sleep or perform light activity like reading, talking, eating or standing watch for most 2 hours
	> if rest is interrupted for more than 1 hour, must restart to gain benefits
	> regain all lost HP
	> regain half max hit dice
	> can only have 1 per day
	> must have at least 1 hp to heal
	
	
LIGHT LEVELS 

- Lightly obscured, dim light, patchy fog, moderate foliage
	> disadvantage on sight perception

- Heavily obscured, darkness, opaque fog, dense foliage
	> blinded condition
